Privacy Policy

This page explains how Gemini Live Test handles data, permissions, and privacy.

Summary

Gemini Live Test does not sell personal information or use personal information for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Overview

Gemini Live Test is operated by Edis Kulaga. The app is designed to help you speak with an AI assistant and save useful memory from those conversations. This policy explains how the app handles privacy.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Information the App Processes

Gemini Live Test processes only the information needed to provide its voice conversation and memory features.

  • Voice input: microphone audio is used when you choose to talk with the assistant.
  • Conversation content: messages, transcripts, and saved memory are used to provide the app's conversation and memory features.
  • Support messages: if you contact us, we use your email and message only to respond.

How the App Uses Information

Voice input, conversation content, and saved memory are used only to provide the app's AI conversation, memory, and recall features. Edis Kulaga does not use this information for advertising, tracking, or selling data.

AI Processing

Gemini Live Test may send voice, messages, transcripts, and saved memory to an AI service or relay server so the assistant can respond and remember useful context. Do not include sensitive information in the app unless you are comfortable having it processed for these features.

Sharing Information

Edis Kulaga does not sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not use personal information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

Gemini Live Test may use service providers for AI processing, backend relay infrastructure, and memory storage. These providers are used only to operate the app's features, not for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data Retention

Saved memory may be retained so the app can recall it in future conversations. You can clear memory in the app where supported. We keep information only as long as needed to provide the app's features or comply with legal obligations.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

Your Choices

You can control microphone access and other app permissions in iOS Settings. You can clear saved memory in the app where supported, or contact us to request deletion of information associated with your use of the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at ediskulaga@gmail.com.
