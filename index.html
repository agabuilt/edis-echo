Privacy Policy

This page explains how Edis Echo handles data, permissions, and privacy.

Summary

Edis Echo does not collect, sell, or share personal information. Use the final GitHub Pages URL as your App Store Connect privacy policy URL.

Overview

Edis Echo is operated by Edis Kulaga. The app is designed to help you create notes by voice and search those notes with AI-powered search. This policy explains how the app handles privacy.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Information We Collect

The app does not collect personal information.

  • Voice notes: notes you create are used only to provide the note-taking features of the app.
  • Voice input: microphone access is used only when you choose to record or dictate a note.
  • Search queries: searches are used only to return matching notes inside the app.

How the App Uses Information

Voice notes, voice input, and searches are used only to provide the app's note-taking and AI search features. Edis Kulaga does not use this information for advertising, tracking, or selling data.

AI Search

The app uses AI-powered search to help you find relevant notes. The app does not collect personal information for this feature. If a future version uses an external AI service, this policy will be updated to explain what data is processed and by whom.

Sharing Information

Edis Kulaga does not sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not use personal information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

The app does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Retention

Because the app does not collect personal information, Edis Kulaga does not retain personal information from the app. Notes remain under your control in the app unless you delete them or remove the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

Your Choices

You can control microphone access and other app permissions in iOS Settings. You can delete notes directly in the app where supported, or remove the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at ediskulaga@gmail.com.
